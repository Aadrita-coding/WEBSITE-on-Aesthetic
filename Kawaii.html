WHAT IS KAWAII AESTHETIC?

The kawaii aesthetic is described as a “cute” culture, heavily influenced from the visual culture of popular Japanese entertainment and style. Being a youthful, lightly feminine aesthetic, primarily used when describing anyone who is very nice and sweet to everyone. Softgirls and softboys often wear pastel colors. Also directly related to the angelcore and animecore aesthetics. Main Colors: pink and pastel
