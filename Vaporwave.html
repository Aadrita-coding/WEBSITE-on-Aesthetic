WHAT IS VAPORWAVE AESTHETIC?

The visual aesthetic or vaporwave aesthetic (often stylized as "ＡＥＳＴＨＥＴＩＣＳ", with fullwidth characters) incorporates early Internet imagery. It also consists of late 1990s web design, glitch art, and cyberpunk tropes, as well as anime, Greco-Roman statues, and 3D-rendered objects. VHS degradation is another common effect seen in vaporwave art. Main Colors: bright neon (pink, blue, and purple)
